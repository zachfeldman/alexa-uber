Instructions for demo:
- Start OpenVPN
- Connect to static.tunnelservers.net from menu at the top of the screen
- `cd ~/dev/alexa-uber && be ruby main.rb` 
- Say, "Alexa, open uber"
- Say, "get me a cab to the liberty bell"
- Say, "yes"

Tabs:
- Lambda: https://console.aws.amazon.com/lambda/home?region=us-east-1#
- Alexa Skills Kit/Developer Console: https://developer.amazon.com/edw/home.html#/skills/list , "Get an Uber" app
